As an open source project we are happy to embrace those that would like to contribute. Contributions do not need to be technical in nature. We need all kinds of people to make this a reality. There are many positive outcomes from contributing to open source projects. In today's market for engineers many employers are looking for this skill on resumes as it shows the ability to collaborate across time zones and work with people from diverse backgrounds. Also, there is the ability to be social and make friends around the world.
