Passion for this App

The OpenShopper app is an inspiration born from the need for great collaboration in the managing and purchasing of goods for the home in modern times. Today with the rising costs of housing and complicated work schedules the need for collaboration on shopping for the home is rising and it is our passion to solve that problem. My wife Jenny and I are both professionals and have trouble coordinating on the purchasing of goods, so this app is a passion of mine to solve our problems, but also the problems of many like minded people.
